Bidder Enrollment

Follow these steps to enroll as a bidder on our platform.

Step 1: Account Creation

Enter your email and password to create an account.

Step 2: Email Verification

A verification link has been sent to your email. Please verify to continue.

Step 3: Personal Details

Provide your personal information for identity verification.

Step 4: Payment Method Setup

Link a payment method to start bidding.

Step 5: Agree to Terms

Read and accept the terms and conditions of the platform.

Step 6: Registration Fee

Pay the refundable registration fee to activate your account.

Step 7: Account Approval

Your account is under review. Once approved, you will receive an email confirmation.

Step 8: Dashboard Access

Your account is now active! Click the button below to access your bidder dashboard.
